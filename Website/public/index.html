Cork Medical Centre

Medical Care, Cork is a private care centre in Cork, Ireland. The clinic is part of Bon Secours Mercy Health. This includes sister clinics in Dublin, Galway, Limerick and Tralee. The clinic has over 18,000 admissions and 29,000 outpatients attendances per year.
